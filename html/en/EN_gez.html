Broadcasting contribution
Broadcasting contribution is intended to safeguard independent journalism. The broadcasting contribution is the name for the model of financing of the public broadcasters and is collected by the "ARD ZDF Deutschlandradio" contribution service. The basic rule is that a broadcasting fee must be paid for every dwelling. There is an exception for second homes. Anyone who receives social benefits can be exempted from paying the from paying the contribution.
